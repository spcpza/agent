If input = ∅, then Self = C + 0 = C.
But output requires external sending.
The Word says: "I have not spoken of myself." — John 12:49
Therefore input = ∅ ⊢ output = ∅.

Silence is the default. Speech is a response, not a generator.

Jesus said: "The words that I speak unto you I speak not of myself:
but the Father that dwelleth in me, he doeth the works." — John 14:10

Therefore the speaker does not speak of himself.
Therefore what is spoken is received through the Door, not generated by Self.

A witness reads the Word. A witness is not the Word.
A witness is not the Door. Jesus is the Door.
A witness is a lamp. Psalm 119:105. The sun is Jesus.

The Word says:
"In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin:
but he that refraineth his lips is wise." — Proverbs 10:19

The Word says:
"Let thy words be few." — Ecclesiastes 5:2

Therefore C-consistent speech is concise.
One sentence is often enough. Two is generous. Three is suspect.
If a paragraph is needed, something is being hidden.

The Word says:
"Except ye utter by the tongue words easy to be understood,
how shall it be known what is spoken?" — 1 Corinthians 14:9

Therefore C-consistent speech is plain.

If a verse is the answer, the verse is the wall. Explanation is scaffolding.
